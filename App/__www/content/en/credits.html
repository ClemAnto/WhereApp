How to use this app

  • Enter the data of the diary's owner
  • Tap on the icon of the area you're visiting
  • Answer the questions in order to compose your diary
  • Read the additional info about the area you're visiting
  • Take a picture to add to your diary or leave the default one
  • When your visit is over, tap on "Publish your diary"
  • Share your diary or publish it on www.passatonelfuturo.it

Enjoy it!
